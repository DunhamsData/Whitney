This interactive visualization allows users to explore data from “Global Architectures: The Alvin Ailey American Dance Theater, City by City and Year by Year, 1958-89,” which is part of the Radical Accounting series commissioned for the Edges of Ailey exhibition at the Whitney Museum of American Art (2024-25). We see AAADT growing from a small touring company to a global phenomenon, as they shared a living history of dance with audiences around the world.
