This interactive visualization allows users to explore data from “Repertory as Living Dance Museum: The Alvin Ailey American Dance Theater’s Performance History, 1958-89,” which is part of the Radical Accounting series commissioned for the Edges of Ailey exhibition at the Whitney Museum of American Art (2024-25). We see Ailey’s vision of repertory as a living museum emerge from the interconnections among every piece, every performance, and every program.
